Profile

『武田鳴』 京都市在住の音楽家。2023年 Bandcampにてデジタルアルバム「Dependency」を発表。以降本格的に活動を開始。
不協和音と電子音響を操り、人間の聴覚神経に直接的な刺激を与える実験的な音楽を制作。
社会の中で疲れ果て、世界に違和感を覚える人たちの心に、不思議な音の刺激で小さな揺らぎを届けている。
暗い部屋で、夜更けに、一人でイヤホンを通して聴いてもらえたら幸いです。

Mei Takeda is a musician based in Kyoto. After releasing his digital EP "Dependency" on Bandcamp in 2023, he began his full-scale musical activities.
Creating experimental music that directly stimulates human auditory nerves through dissonance and electronic sounds. Through mysterious sonic stimulations,
he delivers subtle vibrations to the hearts of those who feel exhausted and alienated in society.
He hopes his music will reach listeners alone in their dark rooms, through headphones, late at night.
